  HOW TO OPEN IN GOOGLE CHROME (STEP BY STEP)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHOD 1: Use START_HERE.html (EASIEST!)
─────────────────────────────────────────
1. Find the file: START_HERE.html
2. Double-click it
3. Click the "START GAME" button
4. Done!

METHOD 2: Direct Open
──────────────────────
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Press: Ctrl+O (Windows) or Cmd+O (Mac)
3. Navigate to: /Users/donovan.mckee29/superhero-dispatch-game/
4. Select: index.html
5. Click "Open"

METHOD 3: Drag & Drop
──────────────────────
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Open Finder (Mac) or File Explorer (Windows)
3. Navigate to: /Users/donovan.mckee29/superhero-dispatch-game/
4. Drag index.html into Chrome window
5. Drop it!

METHOD 4: Right-Click
──────────────────────
1. Find index.html in the folder
2. Right-click on it
3. Select "Open With"
4. Choose "Google Chrome"
5. Click
